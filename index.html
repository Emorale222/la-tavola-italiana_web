Our Story

Located in Bridgewater, NJ, La Tavola is a fine dining experience rooted in authentic Italian traditions and modern culinary precision. Our Executive Chef, Giancarlo Rizzo, trained in Milan and now brings the heart of Italy to Somerset County. We serve handmade pastas crafted daily, seasonally curated rare Italian wines, and fresh local produce in a sophisticated setting that feels like home.
